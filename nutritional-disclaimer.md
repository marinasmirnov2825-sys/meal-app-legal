# Nutritional Disclaimer — MEAL App

**Last updated: March 29, 2026**

## Important Notice

The nutritional information, meal plans, macro calculations, and dietary suggestions provided by MEAL are for **general informational and educational purposes only**.

## Not Medical Advice

MEAL is not a medical application and does not provide medical advice. Nothing in this app should be construed as:

- Medical advice or diagnosis
- A prescription or treatment plan
- A substitute for consultation with a qualified healthcare professional
- Professional dietitian or nutritionist advice

## Consult a Professional

Before making significant changes to your diet, especially if you:
- Have a medical condition (diabetes, heart disease, kidney disease, etc.)
- Are pregnant or breastfeeding
- Are under 18 years of age
- Take prescription medications
- Have a history of eating disorders

**Please consult a qualified healthcare professional or registered dietitian.**

## Accuracy of Nutritional Information

- Nutritional values are approximate and may vary based on specific ingredients, brands, and preparation methods
- AI-generated meal suggestions may contain inaccuracies
- We do not guarantee the completeness or accuracy of any nutritional data

## Allergen Warning

While MEAL allows you to set dietary restrictions, we cannot guarantee that all meals and bundles are free from specific allergens. Always verify ingredients carefully if you have severe food allergies.

## Canadian Food Labelling

Nutritional information in MEAL is provided for reference only and does not comply with the Canadian Food and Drug Regulations requirements for food labelling, as MEAL is not a food product.
